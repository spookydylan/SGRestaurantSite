About Our Pub

We are a traditional English pub located in the heart of the city. We have over 50 beers on tap, including exclusive brews from acclaimed local breweries such as Kurmudgeon and Sl8r. We also serve a variety of delicious pub food, made fresh in our kitchens each day until close. Join us for a drink and a bite!
